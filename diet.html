DIET GOAL

Build Muscle

→ Focus on calorie surplus, protein intake, and strength training

Lose Weight

→ Emphasize calorie deficit, cardio, and portion control

→ Maintain balance with clean eating and hydration

Build Muscle

Focus: Calorie surplus, high protein, strength fueling
Target Calories: 2800-3200 (adjust per body type)
Macronutrient Split: 40% carbs, 30% protein, 30% fats

Daily Routine

Pre-Workout-Snack

  • Banana + peanut butter + black coffee

    • Post-Workout Breakfast

  • 5 boiled eggs + 3 slices whole wheat bread + protein shake

    • Lunch

  • Grilled chicken breast, brown rice, vegetables

    • Snack

  • Low Fat yogurt with honey + almonds

    • Dinner

  • Paneer or tofu + quinoa or roti + salad

    • Night Snack

  • peanut

    • Tips

    • Track calories
    • Drink 4+ liters water daily

    Lose Weight

    Focus: Calorie deficit, nutrient density, portion control
    Target Calories: 1500-1800
    Macronutrient Split: 40% protein, 30% fats, 30% carbs

    Daily Routine

    Breakfast

  • 2 boiled eggs + 1 Bread+ green tea

    • Snack

  • Apple or cucumber slices

    • Lunch

  • Grilled fish/chicken + sautéed vegetables + dal

    • Evening Snack

  • Roasted chana or green tea with nuts

    • Dinner

  • Paneer or Chicken + quinoa or roti + salad

    • Optional

  • Green Tea or Warm Water

    • Tips

    • No sugar or sugary drinks
    • Light walk after dinner (10-15 mins)

    Stay Healthy

    Focus:Balanced eating, whole foods, hydration
    Target Calories: Maintain your TDEE (e.g., 2000-2200)
    Macronutrient Split: 30% carbs, 30% protein, 40% fats

    Daily Routine

    Warm-up

  • Warm water with lemon

    • Breakfast

  • Oats + fruits + nuts

    • Snack

  • Handful of Nuts or smoothie

    • Lunch

  • Mixed dal, rice, sabzi, curd, chicken

    • Snack

  • Buttermilk or fruit bowl

    • Dinner

  • Khichdi or light chapati meal with veggies

    • Tips

    • Stay hydrated with infused water
    • Try 1 fruit & 1 vegetable minimum per day
